About Architecture

Architecture can be defined as the art and techinique os designing and building. Differently than the skills associated with constructions, the practice of architecture fulfills both practical and expressive requirements.

What Do Architects Do?

  • Determine objectives and requirements of projects together with their clients;
  • Calculate and give preliminary costs and construction time to their clients;
  • Direct and supervise both workers who prepare the drawings and the ones responsible for the construction;
  • Prepare scaled drawings of projects meeting the necessary requirements of the client in the best possible way;
  • Manage and prepare contract documents for building contractors.

Important Qualities to Become an Architect

  1. Analytical skills;
  2. Communication skills;
  3. Creativity;
  4. Organizational skill;
  5. Technical skills;
  6. Visualization skills.
